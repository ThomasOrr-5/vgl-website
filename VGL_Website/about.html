About VGL, Inc.

VGL, Inc. has been a trusted provider of skill-based gaming machines to businesses across multiple states. We partner with gas stations, convenience stores, smoke shops, bars, and restaurants to deliver high-quality machines, ongoing support, and transparent revenue-sharing programs.

Our mission is to help business owners increase revenue with minimal effort, providing full-service solutions from installation to maintenance and reporting.

Operating in Georgia, North Carolina, Virginia, Texas, Alabama, Iowa, Wisconsin, Pennsylvania, and Ohio, our multi-state experience ensures consistent service wherever you are.
